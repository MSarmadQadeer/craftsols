About Us

We deliver promising services that contain undivided attention and let our clients get the more of their business through perfectly designed software suites. You are going to enjoy services and support that is too rare these days. Get quick digital transformation with customized and uniquely designed business solutions particular to your offered services.

Our finest business consultants have a unique combination of industry experience and technical prowess required to serve specific requirements of our esteemed customers. We are among the ERP software companies that knows how to implement successful ERP system in various departments to craft ease of doing business not only for the customers’ internal stakeholders but also for the rest of the ecosystem they operate in.

Craftsols works with clients worldwide, in diverse industries, with specific focus on financial, FMCG and IT industries with special focus on Large Enterprise sectors. Our business philosophy is founded on the importance of relationships and a persistent search for greater and mutual accomplishments.

Our Mission

We, at Craftsol Systems, are committed and passionate to empower businesses to grow exponentially by Nurturing Synergies among our employees, partners, shareholders and customers through mission critical financial and other software, exceptional BPO and DT services and digitally disruptive solutions which help them succeed in their essential day to day corporate functions.

Our Vision

To be the most disruptive software, solutions and services provider in the region so that we can delight our employees, create value for our stakeholders and bring financial success for our customers.

Core Values

Integrity

We ensure integrity – always. We remain honest, transparent and committed to doing what’s best for our customers, our employees, our stakeholders and overall, our company. We have zero-tolerance for any political or hidden agendas and / or passive-aggressive behavior.

Energetic

We welcome passion and energy at work as it is the founding block of our core. We appreciate active participation as it is essential to create better results. We encourage thoughtfulness and diversity of opinion in making our important business decisions.

Team Work

We promote empathy and humility in our workplace to help understand and appreciate each other’s problems and work towards solving them as one team. This brings a solid support system within the company which not only enrich their professional but also their personal lives.

Professionalism

As we grow faster, our people grow with us and we take it as a responsibility to give equal change of growth to all our employees in a very professional environment.

Disciplined

This helps us to maintain the performance level by increasing the cohesiveness among various internal teams so that everybody is delivering its part in a tight coordination with other teams on a given project.

Loyalty

Our employees and customers are the most valuable assets for us. We are committed to build and maintain loyal relationships with our external and internal customers.

Innovation

Our culture of constant innovation and continuous improvement in our own business processes helps us seek personal and professional growth. We strive for game-changing innovation in every aspect of our business by inspiring learning creative problem solving for our customers.

Passion

Passion is in our core and we strongly believe in being fearless and objectively forward looking in our professional journey. It helps us in accepting extremely challenging assignments and finishing them in a very professional and skilled manner.

Customer-First Approach

Our mind-set is that the customers are our life-lines. We treat our customers as our family and make sure to maintain all levels of respect, integrity and professionalism. We are committed to our customers and keep the as a top priority in every single business decision making process.

Quality Consciousness
